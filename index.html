DAUATHACHDUA

ABOUT US

DAUATHACHDUA is a non-profit organization created by Khiet Linh with her 5000 dong donation in order to nurture the dream and potential of future generations. This year, the organization welcomes 5 new beneficiaries with unique strengths and weaknesses to prepare them for the upcoming national competition. And here are the 6 current members:

  • Nguyễn Ngọc Châu Anh
  • Kiều Nguyễn Khiết Linh
  • Lê Thanh Nghị
  • Nguyễn Tiến Nhân
  • Trần Đại Nhật
  • Nguyễn Như Quỳnh

In addition, the organization was also founded for an environmental cause. As climate change is deteriorating, countless species of animal are plummeting in number and some are on the verge of extinction. Thus, we choose the Koalas as our mascot not only because of its adorable appearance but also to raise awareness about its rapidly declining population (from between 46,000 and 82,000 in 2018 to between 32,000 and 58,000 in 2021, changing its status from vulnerable to endangered)

With those two purposes in mind, DAUATHACHDUA aimed to provide the most holistic form of education to the 6 koalas with the help of 5(+3) teachers and experts in the field. Moreover, opportunities for relaxation were also presented with frequent visits to a local cafe as well as a 10-day trip to a faraway land in the North (more details in the Events section).

After spending 5 months with each other, countless memories, from the dearest ones to the more disastrous ones, were made and may never fade away. This website serves to somewhat capture the most memorable features and interactions within those 5 months and also to pay tribute to all the "dedication" and "hard work" of the members throughout the period. What lies ahead of the 6 of them, not only academically but also in their personal endeavors as a whole, let us wish nothing but the best for them. I hope you have a great time visiting our website.
